3-Way

Meet our most versatile mount yet. Use it in three main ways—as a grip, extension arm or tripod—for immersive POV and follow-cam footage, static tripod shots and more. The folding arm makes it easy to capture selfies without the mount appearing in the shot. The handle doubles as a grip, and stores a lightweight, mini tripod.
